Position Management

HRMS features Position Management that allows the software to have a central repository for everything related to a particular position eg : Sales Manager. Position Management is an part of HRMS that reduces duplication and redundancy of data across the system. Its an important part of HRMS that lets the client define, designate and track each positionâ€™s roles and responsibilities. It also helps to set unique policies from position to position.

Employee Database

HRMS also has Employee Database that stores all the essential data about your employees. From Financial data to Personal Data to Medical records to Documentation. Employee Database helps you keep all that data on your fingertips. HRMS enables employees to update required data and get it verified by the management.

Employee Digilocker

HRMS also has Employee Document Center also as an official document section that can keep a record of all the official communications done with an employee over the years. A nifty feature that allows HRs to find what they need in a warp speed.

Configure CTC Structure

HRMS allows you to configure all Pay-heads from SCRATCH. Salary structures can be defined on positions which helps to create different CTC structures for different positions. Easily configure Salary structure with formulae which help the system to break up CTC into individual components.

Asset Management

HRMS has a fully-loaded Asset Management. Manage all your assets with the value and invoicing details. Set who is responsible for which asset and who is eligible for which asset. HRMS also has Asset Stock Entry, Allocation with pictures, De-allocation & Damage recovery.

Charts & Analytics

HRMS also allows you to see Real-time data on your fingertips. That means, you can see all your HR data in graphical format for in-depth analysis in blink of an eye. No need to see lengthy reports and waste time every day. Save time, Save Money.
