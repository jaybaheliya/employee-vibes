Know us

Get to know us

EmployeeVibes is the HRMS product of Techlogic Software Services Pvt Ltd. EmployeeVibes defines, automates, and streamlines end-to-end HR Processes with industry-standard compliance to building Value-Based Relations with every employee right from “Recruitment to Retirement”. EmployeeVibes is flexible enough to seamlessly integrate and configure HR processes to meet the requirements of any organization.
